  5. Consultation Call Script & Prep Checklist

    Prep Checklist

    • Confirm appointment details in CRM
    • Print client's property search/profile
    • Review comparable sold listings (for sellers)
    • Prepare market update slides or printouts

    Call Structure (30 min)

    1. Build Rapport (3 min)
      • Warm greeting: "How has your day been?"
      • Quick personal connection.
    2. Set the Agenda (2 min)
      • "Today we'll cover your goals, discuss market conditions, and outline next steps. Sound good?"
    3. Discovery & Trust (10 min)
      • Ask qualifying questions.
      • Listen actively, mirror back concerns.
    4. Market Insights (5 min)
      • Buyers: current inventory, pricing trends.
      • Sellers: recent comparable sales.
    5. Action Plan (7 min)
      • Outline timeline and milestones.
      • Explain process and communication style.
    6. Close & Next Steps (3 min)
      • "Shall we schedule a follow-up meeting?"
      • Confirm preferences and send calendar invite.
